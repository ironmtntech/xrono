Track - Invoice - Pay

Xrono is a mature, open source, time tracking solution for your business. Developed by Isotope11, Xrono is intended to be a core technology for smooth operation of consultancies. Xrono allows team members to bill time against clients, and then admins to easily export invoices and payroll into their accounting systems. Beyond that, it has vacation time management, overtime, client access and notification, and many more features. Xrono is currently under active development, so feel free to check out our support section if you have any questions.

Features

  • Well defined taxonomy for handling a wide range of situations (Clients, Projects, Tickets, Work Units)
  • Easily translate or rebrand to your teams' needs
  • Quickly create new tickets and work units without impeding your work
  • Track Off-Time, with options for company and personal time
  • Easily see how many off-hours you or your team have remaining
  • Real time and daily client updates via email (no surprise bills!)
  • Very Clean User Interface
  • Built for the latest generation of Chrome, Safari, and Firefox
  • Basic IE 8 and 9 support
  • User & Client Access control
  • Built as a web app, so there are no clients to deploy/maintain across your network
  • Robust comment and file attachment system for Clients, Projects, and Tickets
  • Gravatars for users' avatars
  • Robust Invoicing and Payroll systems
  • Helps prevent double or skipped billing/payroll
  • Historical 'time spent' reporting to track what projects and clients your team has been working with
  • Administrative Site settings
  • Comprehensive test suite for continued development
  • Actively developed, quick bug fixes, and feature releases!
